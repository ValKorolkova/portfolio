About me

Hi!

My name is Valeriya Korolkova, and I'm based in Auckland, New Zealand. I am a goal-oriented web designer and developer with solid skills in HTML, Bootstrap, CSS, and JavaScript. Currently, I am actively working with React, which allows me to create dynamic and responsive interfaces.

I have also completed training in Node.js and possess knowledge of Redux, Express, and MongoDB, enabling me to develop fully functional web applications.

I strive for continuous growth and am looking for interesting projects where I can apply my skills and contribute creative solutions. I am open to new collaboration opportunities!
